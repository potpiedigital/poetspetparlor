Poet's Pet Parlor provides the only 24 hour staffed care for your pet in Marquette County. Offering full service grooming, doggie daycare and luxury boarding, in a safe, fun, and homelike environment. At poets pet parlor, we treat your pet like our own.

ABOUT ME & POET

I am the proud owner of 4 mix breed dogs, Oli, Brick, Jovie and Ned, and three cats whom I love with all my heart. They are my family, and companions, and my inspiration for starting Poets Pet Parlor. I began as a dog groomer who wanted to offer cage free options for grooming and as my clientele grew, I was asked to watch and care for many of my clients pets who did not want to leave their pets at a traditional kennel. I expanded my business to include doggie daycare and now pet boarding that is aimed to provide the most homelike environment for your dog as possible. As an alternative to the traditional dog kennel, I have created a safe and fun environment where your dog can romp with friends in indoor and outdoor play yards, sleep in a home-like bedroom, or even sleep in an on-site apartment in bed with staff.

Poet was my first dog and namesake for Poet's Pet Parlor. He was an amazing dog who began my love for animals. He passed away 2 months after the opening of Poets.

DAYCARE

Your dog will enjoy a day of exercise and play while under the attention of a loving and caring staff. Have peace of mind that your dog is safe and happy while you are away.

Rates

1 hour: single dog - $4, second dog - $3, additional dogs - $2

Half day (4-5 hours): single dog - $16, second dog - $12, additional dogs - $8

Full day (7-9 hours): single dog - $24, second dog - $18, additional dogs - $12

Max day (12- 13 hours): single dog - $36, second dog - $27, additional dogs - $18

Activities for your dog:

  • Supervised indoor/outdoor group play
  • Individual play with staff
  • Practice of known commands
  • Individually chosen nap time-your dog sleeps when s/he is tired
  • Socialization with other dogs
  • Love and attention

Daycare can be a great solution for dogs:

  • Who have high energy
  • Who are lonely
  • Who have separation anxiety
  • Who are destructive when home alone
  • Who need special attention
  • Who need some confidence building
  • Or anyone else who needs to release some energy, be social, and have fun.

Requirements:
