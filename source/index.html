DAYCARE

Your dog will enjoy a day of exercise and play while under the attention of a loving and caring staff. Have peace of mind that your dog is safe and happy while you are away.

Rates

4hrs - Single dog ($12), 2nd dog ($9), each additional dog ($6)

Full day - Single dog ($22), 2nd dog ($16), each additonal dog ($12)

Activities for your dog:

  • Supervised Indoor/Outdoor Group Play
  • Individual Play with Staff
  • Practice of Known Commands
  • Individually Chosen Nap Time-Your dog sleeps when s/he is tired
  • Socialization with Other Dogs
  • Love and Attention

Daycare can be a great solution for dogs:

  • Who have high energy
  • Who are lonely
  • Who have separation anxiety
  • Who are destructive when home alone
  • Who need special attention
  • Who need some confidence building
  • Or anyone else who needs to release some energy, be social, and have fun.

Requirements:

  • 2 hour behavior assesment for first visit
  • Current rabies, distemper and bordetella vacination
  • Completed Daycare enrollment form. download here



BOARDING

Marquette's first 24 hour staffed pet boarding facility with cage free options.

Dogs will be required to be vaccinated for Rabies, Distemper and Bordetella. Vaccinations must be adminstered no sooner than one week prior to boarding date. - A titer antibody test will also be admissible with written consent from vet.
